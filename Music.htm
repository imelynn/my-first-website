The music industry in the 1990's was like magic tricks being unraveled.  Many new and emerging single, duo, and group performers grace the music world.  Most noted to have made a difference in the way it was accepted or just the controversy surrounding it was the HIP HOP class.  Also we seen in that era a lot of mixture between each music form, interesting converges such as Hip Hop and Country music.  This site will explore a few of the major divisions of music and highlight some of the major occurrences pertaining to that style.
